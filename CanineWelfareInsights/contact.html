Contact & About

About the Project and the Creator

My name is Efe Ivagba, and I am the sole creator of Canine Welfare Insights for ANSC 3005 (B00909028). This website is the product of my passion for canine welfare and my commitment to integrating scientific research with everyday pet care. Throughout my studies, I have delved into various aspects of dog welfare, from understanding key frameworks like the Five Freedoms to evaluating behavioral and physiological measures of stress. Inspired by personal experiences and rigorous research, I developed this site as a bridge between academic insights and practical applications.

My Motivation

I grew up with dogs and witnessed firsthand the impact of both proper care and neglect. The challenges some dogs face in shelters, from stress and aggression to inadequate training, motivated me to explore how we can improve their lives. This website aims to empower dog owners, shelter workers, and animal welfare professionals with the knowledge to provide better care for our canine companions.

Thank You

Thank you for visiting Canine Welfare Insights. Each step we take to understand and improve the well-being of dogs contributes to a better future for our loyal companions. Let us continue the conversation and work together to ensure every dog lives a happy, healthy life.
